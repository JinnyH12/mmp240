SpongeBob SquarePants

Voice Actor: Tom Kenny

SpongeBob is a cheerful yellow sponge living in a pineapple house in Bikini Bottom. He works at the Krusty Krab, making the best Krabby Patties in town. No matter the situation, SpongeBob always stays optimistic and loves his friends and his life.

Patrick Star

Voice Actor: Bill Fagerbakke

Patrick Star is SpongeBob’s best friend, a pink starfish living in Bikini Bottom. Though often lazy and a bit slow, Patrick is very loyal and always joins SpongeBob on his adventures. He lives under a giant rock and often finds himself in funny situations.

Squidward Tentacles

Voice Actor: Rodger Bumpass

Squidward is SpongeBob’s neighbor and co-worker, often annoyed by SpongeBob’s energy and enthusiasm. He is cynical and grumpy, but he secretly dreams of being a famous musician and loves playing his clarinet.

Mr. Krabs

Voice Actor: Clancy Brown

Mr. Krabs is the owner of the Krusty Krab restaurant, known for his love of money. While he appears greedy at times, he does care about SpongeBob and the other employees deep down. The Krabby Patty is the most popular dish in Bikini Bottom, thanks to him.

Sandy Cheeks

Voice Actor: Carolyn Lawrence

Sandy is a squirrel from Texas who lives in Bikini Bottom in a tree dome. She’s smart, brave, and skilled in science and karate. Sandy often goes on adventures with SpongeBob and helps him out in tricky situations.

Plankton

Voice Actor: Mr. Lawrence

Plankton is a tiny single-celled organism who constantly attempts to steal the Krabby Patty secret formula. He is smart and cunning, but his plans often fail. Despite this, he remains a determined antagonist.

Gary the Snail

Voice Actor: Tom Kenny

Gary is SpongeBob's pet snail, known for his intelligence and gentle nature. He makes sounds similar to a cat and can express emotions with his tentacles. Gary plays a significant role in SpongeBob's life.
