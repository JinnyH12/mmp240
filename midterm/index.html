SpongeBob SquarePants is an animated series created by Stephen Hillenburg that premiered in 1999. It follows SpongeBob, a cheerful yellow sea sponge living in a pineapple under the sea in Bikini Bottom. He works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and dreams of being the best chef. Alongside his friends—dim-witted Patrick Star and grumpy Squidward Tentacles—SpongeBob navigates various humorous adventures, exploring themes of friendship and optimism. The show is beloved by audiences of all ages for its humor and creativity.
