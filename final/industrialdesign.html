Bronx Community College
Program: Digital Design
Degree: A.A.S.
Discipline: Industrial Design
Location: Bronx
Description: This program prepares students for careers in the visual communication industry in positions such as graphic design, web design, computer animation, 3D graphic visualization, motion graphics design and interactive multimedia design.

New York City College of Technology
Program: Communication Design
Degree: A.A.S.
Discipline: Industrial Design
Location: Brooklyn
Description: The AAS degree program in Communication Design provides students with a solid foundation in the theory and practices of the Communication Design profession and a grounding in general education. Students explore visual expression through a range of courses in drawing, design, and web design.

Baruch College

Degree: B.A.
Discipline: Industrial Design
Location: Manhattan
Description: The graphic communication specialization provides training in graphics as well as basic training in the broader aspects of business communication. It prepares the graphic artist to enter a business environment.
